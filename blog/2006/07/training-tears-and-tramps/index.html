Training, Tears and Tramps

By Claire Blackshaw

Okay so I'm attacking things at a furious pace. Not a problem at all.

So anyway the main thing regarding training is I have signed up to an online programming course. This is to fill in a CV gap, not in training but in time. It looks bad to say I havn't been working on programming for over nine months. So its more geared to say "the look" than the training.

However the topic is new and old to me. Old because I've done loads of OpenGL and 3D programming theory. New because I've never got round to fighting with DirectX.

Crazy and no exception.
