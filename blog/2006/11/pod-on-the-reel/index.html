Pod on the Reel

 1 November 2006
By Claire Blackshaw

Oh well another podcast is on the reel. I'm currently editing lots of stuff out and fixing audio levels. We finished recording yesterday, and today I got my pop filters. I CANT BELIEVE THE DIFFERENCE! I'm just kicking myself we didn't have them for the last recording but oh well.

I'm taking everyone's feedback in and to be honest I'm amazed at all the positive critical feedback I got. All constructive and supportive. Please keep it coming guys because we do listen and it helps us improve the show.

This weeks topics were in a bit of confusion but I think we pulled it off in the end. Everything looks on track and the podcast will be released on Friday as planned.

See you all then.
