[72 Hour] Progress Report 3

 72 Hour Game Dev Contest Programming 18 November 2006
By Claire Blackshaw
1 days, 21 hours, 6 minutes, and 47 seconds

So it took me all of 1 hour or so to add, a map editor with loading and saving. Not to mention collision detection and smooth out the kinks in the gravity equation. Oh and did I mention visualize the field.

Then someone says, make it isometric. So happy little programmer I am I say, SURE!

Its now 8 hours later....

My isometric junk is horribly broken, and still not quite working. What is the moral of the story here. Don't try learn something new IN THE MIDDLE OF A COMPETITION! I'm having dinner now, going back to flat. Working out some things then making one LAST attempt at isometric.
