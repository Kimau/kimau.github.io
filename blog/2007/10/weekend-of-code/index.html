Weekend of Code

 Weekend of Code Contest 1 October 2007
By Claire Blackshaw

So this weekend we are having a weekend of code. Basically the competition is simple. You have a weekend to make a game.

The Rules and Hints can be found here.

Final Themes

This is the final list of themes for this weekend. The official theme will be announced at 1800 on Friday (UK time). So without further delaying they are.

  • Braaaaains!: Anything brains related
  • Magnets / Gravity: Attraction and Repulsion
  • Generated Content: No loading of graphics sound or levels. All generated on the fly.
  • Squirrels: The furry little devils
  • Ricochet: Use of rebounds as core element
  • Transform: Transforming or Shape-shifting
  • Construction: Building (please avoid Tetris)
