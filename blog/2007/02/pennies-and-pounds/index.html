Pennies and Pounds

 16 February 2007
By Claire Blackshaw

Oh why oh why do I take my hard earned achievements and rub them into the cake batter for the little monsters of my id to devour. I dot know but I do go all out icing and cherries.

I often find my art be it graphic, verbal or written has a much clearer and far sighted view of my life than my concious disseminations. In particular I think back on my play Identity. There are many lines and moments in it which I find myself self referencing as cautions. I often wish I had a video of it so I could see my message to myself.
